Cape Code Rail Trail

This 25 mile path spans from Dennis to Wellfleet. Bring your own bike, or rent one from a local shop like Barb's Bike Shop

Downtown Provincetown

Take a scenic drive tot he end of the Cape for restaurants, shops, and some of the best people watching on the east coast.

Chatham Center

A seaside chic town town filled with galleries, restaurants, and shops. Be sure to stop at the Chatham Candy Manor!
